Tachymaze

Offline navigation for your long runs and rides.

Use along with your favorite workout app.

Switch easily using the side button.
Tachymaze uses WatchOS session and multitasking features to be the best companion for your adventures.

Follow the green line

Distraction free navigation, follow the green line!
Tachymaze integrates with always on display. Switch to Tachymaze and the display will show you the way until you switch back to your workout app.

Off Route alerts

You will get an alert when you go off route. A blue line will show the location of the closest point on the route.

Offline Map

A snapshot of the map is stored on device for each route. Helps spotting routes and lanmarks around the path.
