Tachymaze

A unique approach to navigation for runners and cyclists

Use with Your Favorite Workout App

Tachymaze is designed to run alongside any workout application.
Double press the crown or use the side button to switch.

Always-On Display

Safe and distraction-free navigation.
A quick glance at the screen will show you your position and trace relative to the route, limiting distraction and risks.

Create Your Routes

Create your route on the phone and transfer it easily to your Apple Watch.

Off Route alerts

You will get an alert when you go off route. A line will show the location of the closest point on the route.

Offline Map

A snapshot of the map is stored on your Apple Watch for each imported GPX file. Helps you spot routes and landmarks around the path.

Bookmark Locations, Display Elevation Profile

Bookmarks include an icon and will be displayed on the navigation screen. Elevation profile can be enabled; this will also display your position on the route.

HR Display, Wrong way indicator

HR display and wrong way indicator can be enabled.
