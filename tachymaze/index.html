A unique approach to navigation for runners and cyclists

Use your favorite workout app !
Tachymaze takes a unique approach to helping athletes follow a route. It is designed to run alongside any other workout app and uses system-level features to offer seamless integration.

Always on display!

Safe and distraction free navigation !
A quick glance at the screen will show you your position and trace relative to the route limiting distraction and risks.

Off Route alerts

You will get an alert when you go off route. A line will show the location of the closest point on the route.

Offline Map

A snapshot of the map is stored on device for each imported GPX file. Helps spotting routes and lanmarks around the path.
